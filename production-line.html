Our Production Lines

Innovative Cosmetic Manufacturing: Precision, Quality, and Scale

At Cristal for Cosmetics, we operate state-of-the-art production facilities designed to meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency. Our production capacity is tailored to accommodate a wide range of product specifications, ensuring we can meet diverse customer needs.

  • Total Production Lines: 12 fully equipped production lines.
  • Manufacturing Mixers: 12 advanced manufacturing mixers.
  • Mixer Capacity: 500kg per mixer, ensuring large-scale production.
  • Production Capacity: Up to 20,000 units per shift.
  • Sachet Production: We offer sachet products through our powerful sachet production line, capable of producing high-quality sachets tailored to your product requirements.
  • Working Hours: 8 hours per shift (with flexibility to adjust filling capacity based on product specifications)

Our flexible production setup allows us to adjust our filling capacities to meet the specific requirements of each product, ensuring the highest quality and consistency.

Water Treatment Station (WST)

At Cristal for Cosmetics, we are dedicated to ensuring that every aspect of our production meets the highest standards of quality and safety. One of the key components in achieving this is our state-of-the-art Water Treatment Station (WST), designed and built to pharmaceutical specifications to guarantee the purity of the water used in our manufacturing processes.

Our advanced water treatment system ensures that all water utilized in the production of our hair care, skin care, sun care, and shower gel products is thoroughly purified to meet the most stringent quality standards. The system is engineered to remove impurities and contaminants, ensuring that the water used in our formulations is of the highest quality.

This guarantees not only the safety and effectiveness of our products but also their consistency and reliability.

To maintain the highest standards of quality and consistency, we conduct a rigorous Qualification Protocol for our water treatment station. This includes testing the water twice per day for chemical analysis and performing microbiological tests twice per week. These comprehensive tests ensure that the water consistently meets the required quality specifications for optimal safety in our products.

By utilizing purified water in all our formulations, we ensure that each product we create delivers optimal performance and meets the highest industry standards. This commitment to quality underscores our dedication to producing premium products that our valued customers can trust.

At Cristal for Cosmetics, the integration of advanced water treatment technology, along with our thorough testing protocols, is just one of the many ways we prioritize the safety and quality of our products. We continue to invest in innovative solutions that support our mission to deliver the best possible cosmetic products to the market.

Our Advanced Laboratory Facilities

At Cristal for Cosmetics, we are equipped with cutting-edge laboratory facilities to ensure the highest standards of quality and safety in our products. Our laboratories are an integral part of our commitment to excellence, staffed by skilled analysts and equipped with high-performance devices to conduct precise and reliable testing.

  • Chemical Laboratory: Our chemical lab is dedicated to performing a wide range of tests to verify the physical and chemical properties of raw materials, packaging materials, and finished products. This ensures that every product meets stringent quality specifications and adheres to industry regulations.
  • Microbiology Laboratory: Our microbiology lab focuses on testing for microbial contamination, ensuring that all our products are safe and meet the highest hygiene and safety standards. Regular microbiological analyses are conducted to monitor and maintain product integrity.
    • Together, these labs play a pivotal role in maintaining product consistency, safety, and performance. By leveraging advanced technologies and the expertise of our dedicated analysts, we guarantee that every product delivered to our customers is of premium quality and meets the highest industry standards. At Cristal for Cosmetics, our laboratories exemplify our ongoing commitment to innovation, precision, and excellence in the cosmetics industry.
